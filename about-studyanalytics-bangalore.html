About Us

Learning Analytics is easy with StudyAnalytics. For us, it isn’t only a statement. It’s a practice.Yes, a practice from last 5 years to train and develop analytical skills in any individual who is interested to build a career in Analytics.

StudyAnalytics is so much more than just another training and development organization. It is an initiative to bring skilled and well prepared resources specifically in the field of Analytics to the hiring managers across industries.


Worldwide, more than 75 million young people are unemployed. On one hand, employers aren’t able to find the skilled people for the jobs and on another hand, graduates passing out from colleges and working professionals looking out for a functional shift are struggling to get into entry level analytical jobs. A mismatch in expected and resulted skills is creating a huge gap in the industry to fill. StudyAnalytics through its unique tailor made and industry centric programs on Business Analytics, Data Analytics, Big Data, Data Science, etc prepare unemployed or underemployed candidates to get jobs in analytics divisions of best of the best organizations.
Our mission is to train the candidates with the analytical skills that industry employers need in 21st century from every Analyst.
